About

My story

I founded Le Bouchon Anglais in 2017.
I am an Englishwoman with a passion for France, French culture, and of course, wine.
I have been living in France since 2003.
After successfully completing Level 3 of the renowned WSET* training, I brought Le Bouchon Anglais to life on November 14, 2017, in Versailles.
Since then, we have had the pleasure of sharing our passion for wine by hosting wine tastings, providing purchasing advice, and supporting professionals working with wine (wine bars, winemakers, etc.) through personalized services (French/English translations, wine/food pairings, etc.).
Based in Versailles, Le Bouchon Anglais brings everything you need to introduce you to the art of wine tasting right to your doorstep.

*WSET, what is it?

The Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET) is the largest training organization in the world in the field of wines, spirits, and sake.
Trusted by the most renowned organizations in the industry worldwide, WSET has been developing and delivering its wine and spirits programs for 50 years.
WSET is now present in more than 70 countries and translated into numerous languages; WSET diplomas are a source of inspiration and excellence for both professionals and enthusiasts. For more informations : www.wsetglobal.com
All courses for the WSET Level 3 qualification were completed at the Ecole des Vins et Spiritueux in Paris.
For more informations : www.ecole-vins-spiritueux.com
