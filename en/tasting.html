Our tastings

What does a wine tasting experience consist of ?

The wine tasting experiences are made to measure for each client. They can be in English or French, or both, in the comfort of your own home, in a specific chosen location, a wine bar, restaurant…
Whether you are an individual or professional, these wine experiences allow the clients to learn more about the world of wine and taste the wines together in an relaxed environment.

Depending on your requests, our wine tasting experiences can cover wines from a specific region (e.g. Burgundy, Tuscany, or California), or a focus on a particular grape variety,(merlot, pinot Noir, Chardonnay…), or even food and wine pairing (wine and Cheese, Wine and Chocolat…).
